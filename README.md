# 📈 Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales.
- West region achieved the highest overall profit.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Sales showed an overall upward trend from 2014–2017.
- California and New York were the highest profit-generating states.
- Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode.
- Furniture category had the highest average discount.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Strengthen marketing efforts for Technology products.
- Replicate successful strategies from the West region.
- Improve profitability in lower-performing regions.
- Optimize discount strategies, particularly for Furniture.
- Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs.
- Enhance Standard Class shipping efficiency.
