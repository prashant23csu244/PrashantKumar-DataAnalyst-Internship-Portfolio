# Exploratory Data Analysis & Business Intelligence

## Objective
Analyze the Superstore dataset to uncover business insights through exploratory data analysis.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab

## Key Findings
- Technology generated the highest sales.
- West region generated the highest profit.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest revenue share.
- Discounts negatively impacted profitability.
- California and New York were the most profitable states.
